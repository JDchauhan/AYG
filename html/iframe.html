Rules:-

  1. During the play user has to choose the correct answer in the given time out of the four options provided.
  2. User can use any lifeline during the game.
  3. Use of more than one lifeline on a question will not be permitted.
  4. After the each right answer a choice to buy used lifelines will be provided.
  5. For buying a lifeline it is necessary that you have
  6. used that lifeline earlier on the session.
  7. Enough money to purchase that lifeline.
  8. The cost of lifeline purchased will be deducted from your total score.
  9. If you leave the game before answering the question / in case of wrong answer / in case of time out no money will be added on your scoreboard. The score would be counted only if you have answered the question correct and on the congratulation page you have click on exit or in case of answering of all questions.
